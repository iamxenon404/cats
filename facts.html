Did You Know?

Genetic Royalty

House cats share 95.6% of their genetic makeup with tigers. They even share behaviors like scent marking and pouncing!

Unique IDs

A cat's nose pad is ridged with a pattern that is unique to them—just like a human fingerprint.

Sleepy Felines

Cats spend roughly 70% of their lives sleeping. That averages out to 13–16 hours a day!

Space Pioneers

In 1963, a cat named Félicette became the first (and only) cat to go to space. She returned safely to Earth!

Olympic Leapers

A healthy adult cat can jump up to six times its own body length in a single bound.

Secret Talkers

Adult cats rarely "meow" at each other. They mostly use that sound specifically to communicate with humans.
