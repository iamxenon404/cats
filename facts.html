Did You Know?

Simple facts about cats

Genetic Royalty

House cats share 95.6% of their genes with tigers. Many behaviors overlap too.

Unique IDs

Every cat’s nose has a unique pattern, similar to a human fingerprint.

Sleepy Felines

Cats spend around 70% of their lives sleeping.

Space Pioneers

In 1963, a cat named Felicette became the first cat sent to space.

Olympic Leapers

A healthy cat can jump up to six times its own body length.

Quiet Talkers

Adult cats rarely meow at each other — mostly at humans.
