Quick Cat Facts

Short and simple insights about cats

Tiger DNA

Domestic cats share 95.6% of their DNA with tigers.

Nose Prints

Every cat has a one-of-a-kind nose pattern.

Sleep Machines

Cats sleep for about 70% of their lives.

First Space Cat

Felicette became the first cat launched into space in 1963.

Big Jumpers

Cats can leap up to six times their body length.

Human-Directed Meows

Adult cats mainly meow at humans, not each other.
