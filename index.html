Why Cats?

From ancient Egypt to modern-day memes, cats have basically been running the show. Whether they are knocking glasses off tables or purring in your lap, life is just better with a cat.

Agility

Playfulness

Mystery

Feline Characteristics

Natural Agility

Built for movement, cats can jump five times their own height with ease.

Playful Instincts

Even as adults, cats use play to keep their hunting reflexes sharp and fast.

Quiet Observers

Cats spend much of their day observing the world from high vantage points.

Cat Games

Cats love to play — and so do we. Here are some popular games cats and their humans enjoy.

String Chase

A classic. Dangle a piece of string and watch your cat go absolutely feral. Hours of entertainment guaranteed.

Box Exploration

Leave an empty box on the floor. Your cat will claim it within 30 seconds. It's not a game — it's a lifestyle.

Laser Pointer

The red dot that shall never be caught. Cats will chase it across furniture, walls, and your sleeping face.

Toy Mouse Hunt

Hide a toy mouse under a blanket and let your cat's inner predator take over. Pounce in 3... 2... 1.
